# Platformalar va to'lovlar bo'yicha qo'llanma

## Upwork da profil ochish

1. **upwork.com** ga kiring.
2. "Sign Up" → "I want to work as a freelancer" ni tanlang.
3. Ism, email, parol kiriting.
4. Profilingizni to'ldiring:
   - Professional title: masalan, "AI-Assisted Content Writer & Translator"
   - Overview: 2-3 jumlada nima qilishingizni ayting.
   - Skills: Writing, Translation, Social Media Marketing, Telegram Bot
   - Hourly rate: $10-$15 dan boshlang.
5. Portfolio qo'shing: `samples/` papkasidan namunalarni yuklang.
6. Identity verification (ID pasport yoki haydovchilik guvohnomasi) talab qilinishi mumkin.

**Diqqat**: Agar profil birinchi marta rad etilsa, boshqa email bilan qayta urinib ko'ring. Profil yaxshi to'ldirilgan bo'lishi kerak.

---

## Fiverr da gig joylash

1. **fiverr.com** ga kiring va "Become a Seller" ni tanlang.
2. Profil va gig yaratish:
   - `fiverr-gigs.md` dan sarlavha va tavsifni nusxalang.
   - 3 ta paket yarating: Basic, Standard, Premium.
   - Teglar: 5 tagacha, masalan `content writing`, `translation`, `social media`.
3. Portfolio rasm qo'shing (Canva dan 1280x769 piksel rasm yaratish mumkin).
4. Birinchi 30 kun ichida kamida 5 ta buyer request ga javob yozing.

**Maslahat**: Yangi sotuvchilar uchun $5-$15 oraliqda boshlang, 5 ta review to'plang, keyin narxni oshiring.

---

## Payoneer akkauntini ochish

1. **payoneer.com** ga kiring.
2. "Register" → "Freelancer" yoki "Professional" ni tanlang.
3. Shaxsiy ma'lumotlarni kiriting (ism, manzil, pasport).
4. Bank hisob raqamingizni ulang (Uzcard yoki bank hisob raqami).
5. Tekshirish (verification) 1-5 kun davom etadi.
6. Payoneer email manzilingizni Upwork/Fiverr ga ulang.

**Muhim**: PayPal va Stripe O'zbekistonda qabul qilinmaydi. Payoneer eng ishonchli usul.

---

## Telegram bot ishga tushirish

1. **@BotFather** dan BOT_TOKEN oling.
2. `requirements.txt` orqali paketlarni o'rnating:
   ```bash
   pip install -r requirements.txt
   ```
3. `.env` faylini `.env.example` asosida yarating va BOT_TOKEN yozing.
4. Botni ishga tushiring:
   ```bash
   python telegram-bot-starter.py
   ```
5. Hosting uchun bepul variantlar: PythonAnywhere, Replit, Render, Fly.io.

**Botni mijozga sotishdan oldin uni o'zingiz sinab ko'ring.**

## Portfolio sahifasini ko'rsatish

1. `portfolio.html` faylini oching.
2. `your@email.com` va `@yourusername` ni o'z ma'lumotlaringiz bilan almashtiring.
3. Brauzerda ochib ko'ring.
4. Mijozlarga yuborish uchun havola: GitHub repo havolasi yoki devinapps.com deploy havolasi.

## Mahalliy mijozlarga chiqish

1. Instagram dan o'z shahringizdagi kichik bizneslarni toping.
2. `local-client-outreach.md` dan xabarni nusxalang.
3. Har kuni 10 ta yangi biznesga xabar yuboring.
4. Javob beruvchilar bilan gaplashib, bepul namuna taklif qiling.
5. Birinchi buyurtmani olib, 50% oldindan to'lov oling.

## Kunlik ish rejasi (eng muhim 3 ish)

1. Har kuni 10 ta yangi mijozga taklif yuborish.
2. 1 ta bepul qiymat yaratish (post, matn, tarjima).
3. 2 soat o'rganish va AI toollarni sinash.
